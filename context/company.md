# Gameball — Company Context

## About
Gameball (gameball.co) is a loyalty and customer engagement platform. We offer loyalty points, referral programs, VIP tiers, 40+ reward campaign types (including gamification: Spin the Wheel, Scratch & Win, Slot Machine, Quiz, etc.), communication campaigns, automation, analytics, and widget customization.

## Platforms
- **Shopify** — App on the Shopify App Store
- **Self-Serve** — API-based, for custom integrations
- **Salla** — App on the Salla App Store (Saudi market)

### General Feature Tier Pattern
- **Free**: Basic features, limited campaigns (3-5 active), 1-2 VIP tiers, no advanced customization
- **Starter**: More campaigns (5-10 active), basic branding, referral customization, some analytics, 7-day reverse trial
- **Pro**: Unlimited campaigns, full branding, advanced analytics, custom actions, all redemption methods, checkout blocks
- **Guru**: Everything in Pro + custom fonts, advanced scheduling, repeatability, SMS, leaderboard, full API access, workspaces

### Pricing Model
Monthly, based on **MRC (Monthly Reachable Customers)** with per-customer overages.
